## Who I Am

I'm Ricardo Ribeiro, a Cybersecurity student from Braga, Portugal. I got into 
this field because I wanted to understand how systems actually work and how 
they break. That curiosity led me to a professional course, and eventually to 
building this space to document the journey.

I'm currently focused on the defensive side. SOC operations, log analysis, 
and network monitoring while also getting my hands dirty with offensive 
tools and CTF challenges to understand the attacker's perspective.

## What I'm Learning

- **Network Defence** - firewall configuration, traffic analysis with Wireshark
- **SOC Operations** - log analysis and alerting with Splunk and Wazuh
- **Offensive Security** - reconnaissance and exploitation basics with Kali Linux, 
Nmap, and Burp Suite

## This Site

This portfolio is a learning journal, not a highlight reel. Every write-up here 
reflects something I actually worked through, including the parts that didn't 
go smoothly. If something here is useful to you, that's a bonus.
