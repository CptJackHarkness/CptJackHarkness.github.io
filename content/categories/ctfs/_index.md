Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges are where I test both offensive and defensive skills in simulated real-world environments. 

This category includes my detailed write-ups for TryHackMe rooms, vulnerable machines, and challenges. While my primary focus is SOC operations and defense, understanding the attacker's mindset is crucial. Working through these machines allows me to see how vulnerabilities are exploited, which ultimately teaches me how to patch and monitor them effectively.

* Step-by-step resolution of CTF challenges.
